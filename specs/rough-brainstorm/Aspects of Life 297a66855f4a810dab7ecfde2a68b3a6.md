## **System Design Notes:**

Each mega-category could have:
- **Monthly focus area** (rotate attention)
- **Core metrics** (2-3 key indicators per category)
- **Weekly rituals** (small actions for each)
- **Quarterly reviews** (deep assessment)
- **Annual audits** (realignment with life vision)

This structure lets you track holistically while preventing overwhelm. You could use a dashboard approach where each category gets a “health score” based on your chosen metrics.
